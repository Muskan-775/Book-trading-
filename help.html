Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy and free:

  1. Click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of any page
  2. Enter your email address, create a password, and choose a username
  3. Read and accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  4. Click "Create Account"
  5. Verify your email address by clicking the link in the confirmation email we send you

Once your email is verified, you can start using BookSwap!

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Click "Login" and then select "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password

The password reset link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on your username or profile image in the top right corner
  3. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
  4. Click the "Edit Profile" button
  5. Update your information and click "Save Changes"

You can change your display name, profile picture, contact information, and notification preferences.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to your profile settings (click your username > My Profile > Edit Profile)
  3. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
  4. Follow the confirmation steps

Please note: Account deletion is permanent and will remove all your listings, purchase history, and messages. Any active transactions must be completed before deletion.

Buying Books

How do I search for books?

You can search for books in several ways:

  • Search Bar: Use the search bar at the top of any page to search by title, author, or ISBN
  • Browse Categories: Explore books by category from the homepage or Browse page
  • Advanced Search: On the Browse page, use filters for condition, price range, location, etc.

Search results can be sorted by price, distance, or recently added.

How do I purchase a book?

To purchase a book:

  1. Find a book you want to buy and click on its listing
  2. Review the book details, condition, and seller information
  3. Click "Buy Now" or "Add to Cart"
  4. Follow the checkout process to complete your purchase
  5. You'll receive confirmation and can track your order in your account

After purchase, you can communicate with the seller through our messaging system.

What payment methods are accepted?

BookSwap accepts the following payment methods:

  • Credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • BookSwap Wallet (store credit)

All payments are processed securely through our platform. We never recommend paying sellers directly outside of BookSwap.

How do I leave feedback for a seller?

After completing a purchase:

  1. Go to your purchase history in your account
  2. Find the transaction and click "Leave Feedback"
  3. Rate your experience (1-5 stars) and leave a comment
  4. Submit your feedback

Honest feedback helps other buyers and helps us maintain a quality marketplace.

Selling Books

How do I list a book for sale?

To list a book for sale:

  1. Click "Sell Books" in the main navigation
  2. Enter the ISBN of your book or search by title/author
  3. Select the correct edition from the results
  4. Fill in details about your book's condition, add photos, and set your price
  5. Choose your shipping options and locations you'll ship to
  6. Click "List Book" to publish your listing

Your book will be visible to potential buyers immediately after listing.

How do I set the right price for my book?

When pricing your book, consider these factors:

  • The condition of your book (new, like new, good, acceptable)
  • Current prices for the same book on BookSwap and other marketplaces
  • The original retail price and how recent the book is
  • Special features (signed copy, first edition, etc.)

Our pricing tool will suggest a range based on recent sales when you create your listing.

What are seller fees?

BookSwap charges a simple fee structure:

  • 10% of the final sale price (excluding shipping)
  • A small payment processing fee (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction)

Example: If you sell a book for $20 with $5 shipping, the fees would be:

  • 10% of $20 = $2.00
  • Payment processing = $0.88
  • Total fees = $2.88
  • You receive = $22.12 ($20 + $5 - $2.88)

Fees are automatically deducted when the sale is completed.

How do I manage my listings?

To manage your book listings:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click your username and select "My Books" from the dropdown
  3. From this dashboard, you can:
    • Edit listing details or photos
    • Adjust pricing
    • Mark items as sold (if sold outside BookSwap)
    • Temporarily deactivate listings
    • Delete listings

You can also view your sales history and pending orders from this page.

Payments & Refunds

How do I get paid for my sales?

When you sell a book:

  1. The buyer's payment is held securely by BookSwap
  2. You'll be notified to ship the book
  3. Once the book is delivered or the buyer confirms receipt, the payment is released to your BookSwap balance
  4. You can withdraw your balance to your linked bank account or PayPal

Payments are typically available for withdrawal within 24 hours after the sale is completed.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

To withdraw your earnings:

  1. Go to your Account Dashboard
  2. Click on "Balance" or "Payments"
  3. Select "Withdraw Funds"
  4. Choose your withdrawal method (bank transfer or PayPal)
  5. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw
  6. Confirm the withdrawal

Bank transfers typically take 2-3 business days, while PayPal transfers are usually instant.

How do refunds work?

If you need a refund as a buyer:

  1. Contact the seller through our messaging system first
  2. If you can't resolve the issue directly, go to your purchase history
  3. Find the order and click "Request Refund"
  4. Select a reason and provide details
  5. Submit your request

Sellers have 48 hours to respond. If they approve, the refund is processed immediately. If they decline or don't respond, our support team will review the case.

Refund eligibility:

  • Book not as described or significantly different from listing
  • Book damaged during shipping
  • Book never received
  • Order canceled before shipping

Refunds are typically processed back to your original payment method within 3-5 business days.

Is there a BookSwap buyer protection policy?

Yes, all purchases made through BookSwap are covered by our Buyer Protection Policy:

  • Your payment is held securely until you receive your book
  • You can request a refund if the book doesn't arrive or isn't as described
  • Our support team will help resolve disputes between buyers and sellers
  • In clear cases where the seller is at fault, we guarantee a full refund

To qualify for buyer protection, all communication and transactions must take place through the BookSwap platform.

Shipping & Delivery

What shipping options are available?

Shipping options are set by individual sellers, but typically include:

  • Standard shipping (3-7 business days)
  • Expedited shipping (2-3 business days)
  • International shipping (varies by destination)

Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are displayed on each book listing before purchase.

For more detailed information, please visit our Shipping Information page.

How do I track my order?

To track your order:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "Purchase History" in your account dashboard
  3. Find your order and click "Track Package"
  4. You'll be directed to the carrier's tracking page

You'll also receive email notifications with tracking updates for your order.

What if my book hasn't arrived?

If your book hasn't arrived by the estimated delivery date:

  1. Check the tracking information to confirm the current status
  2. Contact the seller through our messaging system
  3. If the seller doesn't respond within 48 hours or the issue isn't resolved, contact BookSwap Support

For domestic orders, wait at least 7 days past the estimated delivery date before reporting an item as missing. For international orders, wait at least 21 days.

Returns & Cancellations

What is the return policy?

Return policies on BookSwap vary by seller. Each book listing should indicate the seller's return policy. Generally:

  • Returns are accepted if the book doesn't match the description or is damaged
  • Returns must typically be initiated within 7 days of delivery
  • The book must be returned in its original condition

To request a return:

  1. Contact the seller through our messaging system
  2. If approved, you'll receive return instructions
  3. Ship the book back and upload tracking information
  4. Once the seller confirms receipt, your refund will be processed

How do I cancel an order?

To cancel an order:

  1. Go to your Purchase History
  2. Find the order and click "Cancel Order"
  3. Select a reason for cancellation
  4. Submit your request

Orders can only be canceled if the seller hasn't shipped the book yet. If the book has been shipped, you'll need to follow the return process instead.

Sellers have 24 hours to approve or decline the cancellation request. If approved, your payment will be refunded in full.
