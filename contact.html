Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list a book for sale?

Log into your account, go to your dashboard, and click "Add a New Book". Fill in the details about your book, upload photos, and set your price.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and in some locations, direct bank transfers.

How does shipping work?

Sellers are responsible for shipping books to buyers. We recommend using tracked shipping methods. You can arrange local pickup if both parties agree.

What happens if a book arrives damaged?

Contact our support team immediately with photos of the damaged book. We'll help resolve the issue between you and the seller.

Can I return a book?

Returns are handled between buyers and sellers. If the book doesn't match the description, contact the seller and our support team if needed.
