About BookSwap

Our story, mission, and the community we're building

Our Story

BookSwap began in 2023 with a simple idea: to create a community where book lovers could find new homes for their pre-loved books while discovering affordable literary treasures.

What started as a small local project has grown into a vibrant marketplace connecting readers and book collectors across the country. We believe that every book deserves a second chance to be read and loved again.

Our founder, an avid book collector, was frustrated with the waste created when perfectly good books ended up discarded. BookSwap was born from the passion to extend the lifecycle of books and make reading more accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing waste by giving books multiple lives through our platform.

Affordability

Making reading more accessible by offering quality books at lower prices than retail.

Community

Building connections between readers through the shared love of books.

Literacy

Promoting reading and learning by making books more accessible to everyone.
